Welcome Lego-Master!

Wether you have just built your first lego model or are a very experienced lego master, we will do our best to satisfy your needs.

Brickastle is an independent Lego reseller that provides both brand new and preowned lego models. We buy preowned models from all around the world and offer everything to you in one place.

Lego provides a lot of different building set-series, but as the Castle-enthusiasts we all are, we only focus on the castle-series. The first Lego set in the castle-series was introduced in the 1970`s and this serie are still one of the most popular.

We hope you enjoy your stay and find what you are looking for!

Oh...and don`t forget; We provide a unique list of collectable minifigures for each set!
